# BossBiS

A RuneLite plugin that scans your bank, lets you pick a boss, and suggests the best loadout you can assemble from your owned items.

**v0.0 (Foundation) — work in progress.** Build skeleton only; no calc or optimizer yet.

DPS formulas are ported from the OSRS Wiki's open-source DPS calculator (https://github.com/weirdgloop/osrs-dps-calc, Jayden Bailey / WeirdGloop) under GPLv3. Item and monster data are derived from the OSRS Wiki under CC-BY-SA.
